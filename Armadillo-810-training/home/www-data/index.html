Armadillo Series Features

  • High Performance / Low power consumption
    The employed ARM core CPUs provide performance without heat and the need for a fan.
  • Linux Support
    Employing Linux as the standard operating system provides access to rich software resources and proven stability.
  • Network Ready
    With the combination of the standard LAN interface (10BASE-T/100-BASE-TX) and Linux's TCP/IP protocol stack, network ready devices can be easily developed.
  • Small form factor
    With boards the same size as a floppy disc at the big end and half the size of a business card at the small end, Armadillo are a truly small form factor solution.
